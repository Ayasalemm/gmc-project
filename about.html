About the Virus

COVID-19 was first reported in Wuhan, China on December 31, 2019.The disease causes respiratory illness (like the flu) with symptoms such as a cough, fever, and in more severe cases, difficulty breathing. You can protect yourself by washing your hands frequently, avoiding touching your face, and avoiding close contact (1 meter or 3 feet) with people who are unwell. We invite you to visit this page for check daily updates.
